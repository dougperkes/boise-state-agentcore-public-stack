## Take

The system is trending *toward* the ecosystem this week, and cheaply: two of the strongest items are subtraction-by-adoption — Strands `Limits` retiring a guardrail we were about to hand-build, and `io.modelcontextprotocol/ui` retiring our `experimental.ui` identifier. The one change Phil would notice first is the **Opus 4.7 → 4.8 migration**: fewer-step tool turns and stronger computer-use are visible in the agent loop, and the system-in-`messages` caching allowance quietly clears a blocker on the long-stalled prompt-caching work.
