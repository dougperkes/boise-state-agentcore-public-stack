A production-ready, multi-agent conversational AI platform built on **AWS Bedrock
AgentCore** and **Strands Agents** — for teams who want to run their own AI
assistant on infrastructure they control, instead of renting per-seat access to
someone else's.

<YouTube id="hoYHY1JpCbg" title="AgentCore Public Stack — Introduction" />

## What you get

The platform ships the pieces of a modern conversational AI product, wired
together and ready to deploy:

- **Streaming chat** — token-by-token responses over SSE, with conversation
  history and automatic context management.
- **Multi-agent tools** — agents call tools across several protocols: direct
  functions, the AWS SDK, MCP servers, and remote A2A agents.
- **RAG** — ground answers in your own documents and knowledge bases.
- **MCP Apps** — rich, interactive tool UIs rendered inline in the conversation.
- **Artifacts** — generated documents and code rendered in a dedicated side panel.
- **Voice** — speak to the assistant and hear responses back.

## Why an Internal AI Platform?

Commercial AI subscriptions are convenient, but at scale they lock you into
someone else's roadmap, pricing, and data practices. Running your own platform on
infrastructure you control changes that calculus.
